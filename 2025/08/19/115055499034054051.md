Terminal 终端（Bash）中快速安装 Starship 的预设主题 Gruvbox Rainbow：

```
curl -sS https://starship.rs/install.sh | sh

starship preset gruvbox-rainbow -o ~/.config/starship.toml

sed -i '1s/^/eval "$(starship init bash)"\n/' ~/.bashrc

source ~/.bashrc

```
